# TWPC Service Builder

TWPC staff use this private desktop web application in Chrome or Edge while preparing Sunday services. It replaces a fragile local script and spreadsheet workflow with a shared, audited service record and a permanent song and liturgy library.

The main task is arranging one service at a time, editing its content, checking the complete order, and generating a PowerPoint that follows the church's established 4:3 design. The interface should feel calm, familiar, and operational. It uses the church burgundy sparingly for primary actions and selection, clear validation language, and dense but readable forms.

Public access is limited to sign-in and service health. Staff actions are attributed to a display name. Completed services keep reproducible, immutable source pins and generated revisions.
